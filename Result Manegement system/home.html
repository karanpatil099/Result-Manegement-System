About the University

Established in 1989, Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University operates under the Government of Maharashtra Act 1983 as a statutory State Technical University.

Committed to quality education, research, and development to meet society's and industries' demands.

Our Vision

To become a leading 'Center of Excellence' in Engineering, Technology, and Science with a global outlook.

Our Mission

Providing quality technical education, research, and services to meet societal needs effectively.
